About Us

Welcome to Pizzeria Coco, your go-to mobile pop-up pizzeria for artisanal pizza crafted with passion. We specialize in creating unique, handmade pizzas with high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. Our dough is carefully fermented for 48-72 hours, giving it a light, flavorful crust that enhances the taste of our premium pizza toppings.

We offer unique pizzas that you won’t find anywhere else, like our Seoul Street Pizza, inspired by Korean flavors, featuring kimchi, chewy rice cakes, gochujang glaze, and other bold ingredients. This pizza reflects Emily’s Korean-American heritage. In addition to our specialty pizzas, we also serve classic favorites. We believe pizza is for everyone, so we offer the option to veganize all of our pizzas.

Women-owned and operated, founded by Emily and Christina, we bring our passion for artisanal pizza directly to you at local events and pop-up locations. Our mission is simple: to serve the DC/MD/VA (DMV) community with the finest handcrafted pizzas made with love and the best ingredients available. Join us at our next pop-up, and taste the difference in every slice!

Who is Coco?

Pizzeria Coco is named in honor of Christina’s beloved dog, Coco, who passed away at the beginning of 2024. Coco wasn’t just any dog—she was a certified pizza lover, always ready to enjoy a tasty pizza crust as a special treat. In fact, we considered her our #1 pizza fan. When we started this business, we vowed to make Coco proud. Her love for pizza continues to inspire us every day, and we carry her spirit with us in every pizza we serve.
