About Us

📸 @overworkedasian

Pizzeria Coco is the premier mobile pizza caterer serving the DC, Maryland, and Virginia (DMV) area. We specialize in artisanal, handcrafted pizza made with high quality, locally sourced ingredients. Our dough is made from scratch using unbleached, non-bromated flour and undergoes a long fermentation process to create a light, flavorful crust that perfectly complements our premium, fresh toppings.

We offer unique gourmet pizzas you won’t find anywhere else, like our Seoul Street Pizza - a Korean-inspired pizza featuring our house-made kimchi, chewy rice cakes, gochujang glaze, and bold flavors that reflect Emily’s Korean-American heritage. We also serve classic favorites and provide vegan-friendly pizza options so everyone can enjoy our craft.

As a women-owned and operated business, we bring our passion for handcrafted pizza to corporate events, weddings, private parties, and pop-ups throughout the DMV. Find us at local events in Washington, DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland, or book Pizzeria Coco for your next catering event.

Join us at our next event, and taste why we’re the best mobile pizza experience in the DMV!

Who is Coco?

Pizzeria Coco is named in honor of Christina’s beloved dog, Coco, who passed away at the beginning of 2024. Coco wasn’t just any dog - she was a certified pizza lover, always ready to enjoy a tasty pizza crust as a special treat. In fact, we considered her our #1 pizza fan. When we started this business, we vowed to make Coco proud. Her love for pizza continues to inspire us every day, and we carry her spirit with us in every pizza we serve.
