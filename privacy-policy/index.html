Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pizzeria Coco ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a catering inquiry through our website, we collect the contact information and event details you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, and information about your event.

We also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics, which may include your browser type, device, pages visited, and general location (city/region level). This data does not identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your catering inquiry and provide a quote
  • Communicate with you about your event
  • Improve our website and services using analytics data

We do not use your information for any unrelated marketing purposes without your consent.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google may collect anonymized data about your visit. You can learn more at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our business or others.

4. How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain catering inquiry information for as long as necessary to respond to your request and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping purposes. If you would like your information deleted sooner, please contact us (see Section 6 below).

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies through Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect some website functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your information
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
